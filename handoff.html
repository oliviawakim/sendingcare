Color

For our colors, we chose the traditional colors that are representative of Chick-fil-A’s brand, such as the Chick-fil-A red and white, so customers can easily associate our campaign with the original brand. We also utilized Chick-fil-A’s secondary palette to compliment the primary palette and maintain a unique look and feel.

Packaging design

For packaging, we built out unique designs for occasions that customers can send care to others: Birthday, Celebration, Just Because, Love, and Thank You. The occasion stickers are for sealing the Chick-fil-A meal bags to ensure freshness and incorporate a thoughtful element. The boxes contain special gift packages customers can customize, and the sleeves can be slid onto the boxes for a unique touch.
